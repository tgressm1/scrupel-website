Track what you're actually running.

Scrupel is a dose-tracking app for peptides and compounds — protocols, injection-site rotation, lab results, and interaction checks, all in one place. Your data stays on your device.

Request early access See what it does

Protocols, not spreadsheets

Build a protocol per compound — dose, frequency, route, titration schedule — and Scrupel tells you what's due today.

Injection-site rotation

Tracks where you last injected and how long each site needs to rest, so rotation happens automatically instead of by memory.

Lab results on the same timeline

Log bloodwork alongside your dose history and see trends the same way your provider would — never a diagnosis, always context.

Interaction awareness

A research-backed ruleset flags known interactions and cumulative risk across your stack and any prescription medications.

Your data stays yours

Your protocols, doses, and lab entries are stored only on your device. A few optional features — like lab-report import and voice search — send specific content off-device to do their job. See the full Privacy Policy for exactly what happens and when.
